Salman Khan, the 'Bhaijaan' of Bollywood, has given us countless blockbusters! But which ones truly ruled the global box office? Starting our countdown at number 5, it's the opulent family drama, 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' from 2015, grossing over ₹400 crore worldwide! Next up at number 4, the recent espionage thriller 'Tiger 3' from 2023, roaring past ₹466 crore worldwide! At number 3, another action-packed ride, 'Tiger Zinda Hai' from 2017, which captivated audiences globally, crossing ₹565 crore! Our runner-up, at number 2, is the sports drama 'Sultan' from 2016! This wrestling epic earned a massive ₹623 crore worldwide! And finally, claiming the top spot as Salman Khan's highest-grossing movie worldwide, it's the heartwarming 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' from 2015! With its emotional story, it truly conquered hearts and the box office, raking in an incredible ₹969 crore! There you have it – the undisputed kings of Salman Khan's box office reign!
